Paper Presentation

Innovations is the route map for inventions and inventions are the key to the better world. Do you have those unique key of ideas? We are here to hear your ideas in the form of Paper Presentation.

Duration: 10mins

Maximum Participants: 3

Topics:

  • Cloud computing
  • Big data
  • Internet of things
  • machine learning
  • Robotics
  • Virtual reality
  • Cyber security
  • Sixth sense technology
  • 5G wireless technology
  • Blue brain technology
  • Deep learning
  • cryptocurrency and blockchain
  • Image processing
  • Augmented reality
  • network security
  • Nano computing

Rules

Round one: Online Submission

  • Team can consist of maximum 3 members.
  • Only provided topics should be taken for the presentation.
  • The paper must be in IEEE two-column Format.
  • The paper should be in .doc or .docx format.
  • The paper should not exceed 4 pages.
  • Teams can submit the papers electronically at tekzion2k18@aceatech.com
  • Teams should mention their full details in the paper. (Name of the Authors, Departments, Phone numbers, College, etc.,).
  • last date for full paper submission : 10.3.2018
  • initimation for selection for paper presentation:13.3.2018

Round two: Presentation

  • Papers and teams selected after scrutiny of papers will be notified through e-mail
  • The teams shortlisted after round 1 will be required to present the paper.
  • The main event will be an on-stage event.
  • The time limit for presentation is 8 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for Query.
  • The oral presentation should be in the form of Microsoft power point slides which contains the brief of your paper, not to exceed 10.
  • Qualified teams should bring one soft copy (preferably in a pen drive) and 2 hard copies of the technical paper on the day of the event.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
